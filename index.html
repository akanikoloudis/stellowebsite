Thassos, Greece

A gathering place
for those who live
close to the sea

Stello is an open-air restaurant on the edge of Thassos island. We cook with the morning's catch, the island's soil and an honest love of feeding people well. Simple food in a place that takes your breath away.

The Place

Where the
Aegean is
always in view

Every seat at Stello faces the water. Eat beneath the open sky as the light shifts from noon white to the deep amber of late afternoon.

There is nowhere else on Thassos quite like this.
